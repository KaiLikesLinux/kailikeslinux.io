Intro

I am Kai Lyons. I am commonly known as KaiLikesLinux, or KaiLikesProgramming depending on the social media platform you find me. I am non-binary, a Linux user, with passion and zeal for computers. My work is mostly comprised of front-end applications. I do work in back-end environments as well.

Current projects can be found on my work page

Work

The major projects that I am working on are on GitHub, although the newest code is kept private until it is confirmed to be in use. The projects I am currently developing are Vcord, Discord-C-Library, DiscordASM, and Thunux. Previously I was working on Keter Language, which had development suspended until further notice.


The projects of Discord-C-Library, Vcord, and DiscordASM are language learning projects until I get working on large-scale applications, either open-source or closed source for enterprise. All of them are libraries dedicated to talking to the Discord API through different languages of which are C, V, and Assembly. These are languages that I want my work to comprise of.


The Discord Libraries

Vcord, as a library, was determined to be a continuous project. The library is one of the multiple libraries for the newer V programming language. A potential third sister project is in planning, dedicated to the Odin programming language. I also frequently work with the developers of other libraries to get a better product.


The other two libraries are provisional projects. These projects have a limited intention to be maintained after the initial full release. DiscordASM, a Discord API library for assembly language, is not assured to begin development after the Discord-C-Library release due to possible complications in which the assembly language might not be sufficient. The Discord-C-Library, on the contrary, is in development with a higher chance of continuation depending on the community.

About

I am Kai, a 16 years old who lives in Colorado Springs, CO. I use Linux [Usually using Ubuntu or Arch depending on the situation], and FreeBSD. I most commonly use C, C++, V, Golang, Python, and JavaScript as my primary development languages. I am both a closed source and open-source developer known for projects such as Kamojipy, Thunux, Keter Language, among others.


My work takes place on an HP Notebook 14. My laptop currently runs Ubuntu 19.10 from the Mini ISO installer. My primary tools for development are Visual Studio Code as my code editor, the GNOME terminal emulator, Flameshot for use in screenshots, and Firefox for web viewing. My laptop runs with a dual-core CPU and four gigabytes of RAM.
